# Product

## Register

product

## Users

YC Search serves people exploring the startup market: tech workers evaluating companies to join, startups selling into other startups, and analysts looking for company, founder, vendor, and category intelligence. They are usually trying to understand a space quickly without opening dozens of tabs across YC, LinkedIn, company sites, databases, and search results.

## Product Purpose

YC Search is a startup discovery and company intelligence platform focused on the YC ecosystem. It helps users search by idea, company, founder, batch, market, vendor, and adjacent category, then explore results through tables, charts, network views, company detail panels, and an AI-native conversational interface.

## Brand Personality

Modern, analytical, exploratory. The product should feel like a more visually ambitious company intelligence platform: dense enough for serious research, but more fluid, conversational, and generative than traditional database tools.

## Anti-references

Avoid legacy database software, generic SaaS dashboards, CRM-heavy visual language, static spreadsheet-like research flows, and chat interfaces that feel bolted on. The product should not feel like a landing page pretending to be a tool.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with exploration, not lookup.
2. Make the chat interface a first-class control surface.
3. Treat visualizations as navigation, not decoration.
4. Support serious desktop research while making mobile chat feel native.
5. Reveal company intelligence progressively: company, founders, vendors, category context, and related startups.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Preserve keyboard access for search, chat, filters, tables, and company panels. Respect reduced motion. Use color as reinforcement, not the only signal, especially in charts and network views.
